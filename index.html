Introduction

There are many good meditation practices, and a particular type is inner meditation. Unlike some techniques that use music or imagination of external images to help meditating, inner meditation emphasizes focusing on internal parts of a human body, watching to the parts and listening to the parts, namely, concentrating inward on something, not outside. It applies few fundamental techniques including breath, concentration, relaxation, body postures, and mental power to develop life energy, or qi. The development of qi inside one's body enhances overall wellness of health and meanwhile leads to a path of spiritual enlightenment.

Bringing all attention inward will cause profound changes to our body and mind gradually. It develops a human's awareness. Being aware of internal states of the body and mind is of great power, because it represents the starting point of transforming one's negative emotion to positive energy. When combined with conscious will power, the awareness is one of the most effective ways to cultivate life energy or qi; the qi in turns enhances the awareness. Of course, it also has relatively well-known benefits that one can obtain through meditation such as inner peace and enhanced concentration power.
