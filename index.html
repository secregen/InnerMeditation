Awakening Healing Light of the Tao, Back to the Body's Wisdom

In this workshop, Master Chia teaches fundamental practices of Taoist meditation. He guides you through each step and helps you activate the internal flow of your life energy, opening up your microcosmic orbit. This inner energy flow radiates and generates healing power inside your body. Afterward, he leads you to go back to your body's wisdom which is activated and developed by Inner Smile--smiling to your organs in the creating cycle. This natural wisdom will make your organs healthy and strong and grow your soul spirit. Besides, he teaches the Six Healing Sounds, which is the emotional wisdom that gives you control of your emotions which would otherwise be difficult to manage.

Loving Energy that Heals

Mantak Chia has been famous for teaching Healing Love for decades since the 1970s. Take this opportunity to learn these practices that were once mysterious from the legendary master face-to-face. Love-making or sexual behavior generates tremendous life energy, which ordinary people have no way to control. He teaches you how to manage your sexual power and transform it into incredible healing energy that flows through your body and blows up your sensory nerves. In this workshop, he guides you through the process of balancing Yin and Yang, ranging from connecting the sexual power with universal human love to harnessing sexual energy from both male and female.

Fusion of Five Elements & Brain Wisdom

Your energy centers vibrate, and your energy channels are open after four days of practices with Master Chia. Now, it is time for advanced meditation that fuses all types of energy inside and outside of your body, and use them to activate your brain. Fusion of Five Elements is one of the best Taoist practices that balance all essential energies in your body, dissolving both psychological conflicts and physiological imbalance. It brings all types of internal energy into harmonious forces and prepares for advanced Qi cultivation and transformation. Also, he teaches you how to use the balanced power to awaken the wisdom of your brain, which is the result of seemingly intuitive brain activities, not the outcome of conventional logic thinking.
